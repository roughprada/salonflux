SALON FLUX is an artist run organization based in London that seek to support and promote emerging practices to audiences from different cultural and social backgrounds.

Salon Flux - Publishing & Archive

After five years of books production/collaborations and events we are keen and confident to start working with practitioners who can see books as a starting point for endless reading possibilities based on photography.

The Archive aims to support the self-publishing
and photobook scene by providing a not-for-profit physical and digital space to distribute and promote publications across London.
